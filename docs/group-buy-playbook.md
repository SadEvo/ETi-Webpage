# Elite Ti — Group Buy Playbook

Group buys are our single best revenue tool (the SC300/SC400 door card pre-order alone did
**$31K / 24 orders**). This is the repeatable system so any group buy runs the same proven way.

## Why group buys work for us

- **High-ticket, made-to-order parts** — pre-orders let us validate demand and fund production
  before committing capital.
- **Scarcity + deadline + community** — this audience commits when there's a clear window and
  they see others joining. Tier unlocks turn buyers into recruiters ("we're 3 away from the
  next price drop — tag a friend").
- **Captures big-ticket money in waves** — pairs perfectly with proven windows (tax-refund
  season, post-holiday build season).

## The mechanics

### 1. Pricing tiers (MOQ unlocks)
Reward early + collective commitment. Example structure (adjust per product):

| Tier | Trigger | Buyer price |
|---|---|---|
| **Founding / Early Bird** | First N orders OR first 72 hrs | Lowest price |
| **Group Buy** | Standard window | Mid price |
| **List** | After the buy closes / in-stock | Full list price |

> For the SC300/SC400 buy, the early group-buy participants already got the lowest pricing.
> This round runs at **list**: ~$900 door trims / ~$1,400 door cards. Frame it honestly —
> "early-buy pricing has closed; this is your last chance before retail/in-stock pricing."

### 2. Minimum order quantity (MOQ)
Set the unit count needed to greenlight production. Show progress publicly ("18 / 25
committed"). If MOQ isn't hit by the deadline, either extend once or refund deposits.

### 3. Deposit vs. paid-in-full
- **Deposit model** (e.g. 50% down) lowers the barrier on $1K+ items and widens the funnel.
- **Paid-in-full** simplifies accounting and signals serious demand.
- Recommend **deposit** for new/unproven products, **paid-in-full** for repeat buys with a
  known fulfillment timeline.

### 4. Timeline (the 4-beat arc)
| Beat | Timing | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Tease** | 1–2 weeks before | Spec sneak peeks, "something's coming," collect email/SMS sign-ups for early access |
| **Launch** | Day 0 | Open pre-order, post everywhere (see distribution plan), email/SMS blast, pin in groups |
| **Mid-point** | ~Halfway | Tier-unlock / MOQ progress update, customer questions, "X away from next tier" |
| **Last call** | Final 48 hrs | Countdown, scarcity, final email/SMS, "closing Sunday" |
| **Close + follow-up** | After | Thank-you, production timeline, fulfillment content, "missed it? join the list for the next run" |

### 5. Set expectations on lead time
State production + ship timelines up front on the product page and in every post. Reduces
support load and chargebacks on made-to-order items.
