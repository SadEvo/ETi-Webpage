# Elite Ti — Marketing Execution Plan (Two-Tier Operating System)

**Purpose:** turn marketing into a repeatable machine with a clear division of labor between a
**human creative agent** and an **AI positioning/distribution agent**, plus a catalog-spanning
email/SMS program that's pre-built so campaigns fire on schedule.

> Companion document: **Go-To-Market Promo & Discount Calendar** (the *what we sell, when, and
> at what price* report). This document is the *how we operate and where we post* report.

---

## 1. The two-tier model at a glance

| | **Tier 1 — Human Agent (Creative)** | **Tier 2 — AI Agent (Positioning & Owned Channels)** |
|---|---|---|
| Who | Current social marketer (the ~$100/mo creative hire) | Claude / sub-agent, supervised by **Luna** |
| Owns | **Making the assets + native posting** | **Positioning, distribution, email/SMS, attribution** |
| Does | Photos, carousels, Reels/TikToks, captions; posts natively to FB, IG, TikTok (+ new platforms) | Klaviyo email/SMS, reshares social into the right groups, Reddit/forum posting via desktop browser, UTM tracking, reporting |
| Does **not** | Decide strategy, discounts, or where to distribute beyond the main pages | Shoot/edit creative |
| Reports to | Luna / Nate | Luna / Nate |

**The core idea:** the human is a **creative engine**, not a strategist. The AI agent is the
**distribution + positioning engine** that takes her finished assets and gets them in front of
*actual buyers* — through owned email/SMS and by syndicating into model-specific communities.

## 3. How the AI agent "reacts" — its operating system

The AI agent runs on a **trigger → action** model so it's never idle and never freelancing.

### Standing weekly loop
1. **Monday — Plan:** pull the promo calendar, confirm this week's campaign(s), draft the
   email/SMS + the group/forum syndication plan, hand the human agent a shot list.
2. **Tue–Thu — Distribute:** as the human posts natively, the AI reshares each post into the
   matching model groups, posts value-first to Reddit/forums, and schedules the Klaviyo send.
3. **Friday — Measure:** compile the weekly report (revenue by UTM, list growth, what landed),
   flag what to double down on next week.

### Event triggers (it reacts to these)
| Trigger | AI agent action |
|---|---|
| New product / group buy goes live | Build launch email+SMS, write group/forum posts, generate UTM links, brief human on creative |
| Promo code approved (from calendar) | Schedule the full send sequence + group syndication, set reminders for last-48hr push |
| Container/restock lands | "Back in stock" flow to waitlist + the model's buyers, group posts |
| Cart/checkout abandoned | (Automated Klaviyo flow — see §6) |
| Campaign ends | Pull results, log to report, archive the code |

### What it uses a desktop browser for
- **Resharing** the human's IG/FB posts directly into the relevant **Facebook/Instagram
  groups** (model-specific) from the brand pages.
- **Reddit** posts/comments in r/RX7, r/supra, r/Nissan GT-R, r/350z, r/JDM, etc. — value-first,
  not spam (see guardrails §9).
- **Forum** group-buy threads and vendor sections (rx7club, supraforums/mkiv, clublexus, GTR
  forums, EvolutionM, etc.).

consistent logo bug, on-brand color, captioned (sound-off viewing). Give her a Canva Brand Kit
so output is consistent without her making brand decisions.

## 6. Catalog-spanning Email & SMS program (Klaviyo) — pre-built

This is the engine that monetizes the whole catalog, not just the hero models. Set it up once;
it runs forever.

### A. Always-on flows (build first)
| Flow | Trigger | Why it matters at ~$1,000 AOV |
|---|---|---|
| **Welcome / new subscriber** | Signs up (popup, link-in-bio) | Captures the build-researcher early |
| **Abandoned cart** | Adds to cart, no checkout | Highest-ROI flow for high-ticket |
| **Abandoned checkout** | Reaches checkout, no purchase | Recover big orders |
| **Browse abandonment** | Views product, leaves | Model-aware: "still eyeing the FD hood?" |
| **Post-purchase** | Order placed | Install tips, cross-sell same-model parts, ask for UGC |
| **Win-back** | No purchase in 90/180 days | Re-engage past buyers |
| **Back-in-stock / waitlist** | Restock or container lands | Pairs with container sales |

### B. Segmentation (so emails are relevant across the catalog)
Segment by **platform interest** using collection/tag behavior — RX-7 buyers get RX-7 emails,
GTR buyers get GTR emails, etc. Key segments:
- Per-model buyers & browsers (RX-7, Supra MK4/MK5, GTR R32–R35, Evo, 350Z/370Z, GT86, RX8,
  Honda, BMW, Tesla, Porsche, Mitsubishi, SC300/400…)
- Universal/Titanium-hardware buyers (cross-platform upsell)
- High-value buyers (>$2,000 lifetime) → early access to drops & group buys
- Engaged non-buyers → nurture

### C. Campaign calendar (broadcasts)
One **catalog-wide newsletter** every ~2 weeks + the **promo sends** from the Go-To-Market
calendar. Rotate the model spotlight so every major platform gets featured each quarter. Each
promo = a 3-touch send: **launch → mid-point/tier → last 48 hrs**, plus SMS on the open and the
final day.

> **Why this is urgent:** owned email/SMS is currently near-zero, yet it's the cheapest way to
> reach *people who already want our parts*. For a $1,000-AOV store this is the single highest-
> ROI addition.

## 8. Attribution — stop flying blind

Today most revenue shows as "direct/unattributed" and social is one giant bucket. Fix it:
- **UTM-tag every link** the AI agent distributes:
  `?utm_source=<platform>&utm_medium=<social|forum|email|sms>&utm_campaign=<promo-code>`
- One campaign tag per promo (matches the discount code, e.g. `sevens-day-2026`).
- This turns "$117K social" into per-platform, per-campaign numbers so we double down on what
  actually converts (likely Groups + Email).

## 9. Guardrails

- **Brand voice:** enthusiast-to-enthusiast, technical, no hype-spam. The AI drafts; Luna/Nate
  approve anything new the first time.
- **Group/forum etiquette:** respect each group's vendor rules. Lead with value (build content,
  tech), not just "buy now." Space posts; don't blast the same copy everywhere same-hour.
- **Reddit:** follow each sub's self-promotion ratio; participate, don't just advertise. A ban
  there is permanent and costs us a channel.
- **Discounts:** only codes from the approved promo calendar, always with a hard end date.
- **Approvals:** Nate approves discount codes; Luna approves email/SMS sends; first-time creative
  formats get a spot-check.
