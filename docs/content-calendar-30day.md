# Elite Ti — 30-Day Content Calendar (late June → late July 2026)

> A day-by-day plan that fixes the #1 reach killer: **inconsistent posting + too much
> "buy now."** Covers our two locked events — **SC300/SC400 Door Card Group Buy** (launch end of
> June) and **Sevens Day 7/7** (run July 5–9) — using the content-pillar mix from the
> distribution plan. Built for ~5 posts/week on IG, mirrored into Groups/forums + email/SMS.

## How to use this

- **Reels and UGC are the reach engine** — product-hero posts convert but don't travel. Note how
  few pure "buy now" posts are below; that's deliberate.
- Every event link is **UTM'd** (see `utm-link-library.md`).
- Group/forum + email/SMS beats follow the **4-beat arc**; this calendar is the IG/feed spine.
- Pillars: 🆕 New drop · 🚗 Build/UGC · 🔥 Group buy/event · 🔧 Tech/install · 🏭 BTS · 💬 Community.

## Reminders that drive reach (not just posting)

- **Post Reels in the first half of the week, hero/carousels later** — Reels do the reach lifting.
- **Reply to every comment/DM within the first hour** — early engagement velocity is what the
  algorithm rewards; this directly attacks the low-reach problem.
- **Stories daily** even on "off" days (polls, countdowns, reposts) — keeps the account active so
  feed posts aren't demoted.
- **Repost customer cars (UGC) relentlessly** — highest trust + free reach in car communities.
- **Every link UTM'd**, every event mirrored to Groups/forums + email/SMS — the feed is only one
  leg of the 4-beat arc.
