# Elite Ti — 12-Month Event & Sales Calendar

**Goal:** at least one major promotable event every month, with a second smaller "flash /
engagement" beat whenever possible. Big swings are deliberately loaded into our proven spike
windows (tax-refund Feb–April, Black Friday Nov, and August).

## Event types (the building blocks)

| Type | What it is | Cadence | Revenue role |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Group Buy** | Pre-order with tiered pricing + MOQ unlocks. New or made-to-order product. | ~Quarterly major | Biggest revenue driver. See playbook. |
| **Model Day** | % off for one platform on a themed date (e.g. Sevens Day for RX-7). | Monthly-ish | Engagement + clearing in-stock inventory |
| **Seasonal / Retail** | BF/CM, holiday, Memorial Day, tax season. Storewide or category. | Tied to calendar | Predictable spikes |
| **Event Tie-In** | Content + sale around an industry moment (SEMA, Tokyo Auto Salon). | A few/year | Reach + hype, content fuel |
| **Flash / Engagement** | 48–72hr restock, bundle, giveaway, or last-call. | Filler beats | Keeps the audience warm between big events |

## Pricing guardrails (high-AOV, considered purchase)

- We do **not** need deep discounts. 10–15% off, bundle value, or group-buy tier pricing is
  plenty — buyers commit on product, not on coupons.
- Protect margin: prefer **threshold offers** ("X% off orders over $1,000"), **bundles**, and
  **group-buy tiers** over blanket storewide percentages, except for Black Friday.
- Always set a **clear deadline**. Scarcity + a countdown is what converts this audience.

- 🔵 **"Mad March" mid-month flash** — restock + bundle on the season's hottest sellers.

### April 2027

## Operating rhythm

- **Quarterly:** Nate locks the next quarter's primary events + which products/group buys feed them.
- **Monthly:** marketing builds the content calendar for the month's primary + secondary event.
- **Weekly:** one "what are we promoting and where" stand-up note (can be async in Metricool/Slack).
- Every event runs the **4-beat arc**: Tease → Launch → Mid-point update (tier unlock / last
  48hr) → Close + follow-up (fulfillment content, "missed it? join the list"). Details in the
  group-buy playbook.
