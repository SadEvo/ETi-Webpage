# Elite Ti — Why Engagement Looks Low (and What to Actually Measure)

> Written in response to: "why are we simply not getting engagement on Facebook/Instagram?"
> Symptom reported: **low likes/comments + low reach/views.**

## TL;DR

For a **$1,000+ AOV JDM parts brand**, raw likes were never going to be high — our buyers
research and commit, they don't double-tap. But **low *reach*** is a real, fixable problem, and
it's almost always caused by the same few things below. The fix is not "post more selfies" — it's
**feed the algorithm content it can distribute, post consistently, and stop measuring the wrong
number.**

---

## The honest diagnosis (from our own data + plan)

| # | Likely cause | Evidence in our own docs | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **We measure likes, not the metrics that matter for high-ticket** | A huge chunk of revenue is *direct/unattributed* — buyers lurk, then DM/type the URL. Public engagement was always going to look quiet. | Re-baseline KPIs around **saves, shares, DMs, profile visits, and revenue** (see table below). |
| 2 | **We're flying blind — no attribution** | Distribution plan §5: no UTM tags, Shopify lumps everything into one "$117K social" bucket. | UTM-tag everything → `docs/utm-link-library.md`. Free. Do this first. |
| 3 | **Reach dies in the gaps** | Plan targets 4–6 posts/week; the algorithm punishes inconsistent posting and demotes accounts that go quiet between launches. | Run the 4-beat arc on a fixed cadence → `docs/content-calendar-30day.md`. |
| 4 | **Wrong content for reach** | Catalog/product shots don't travel. **Reels + UGC (customer cars) + saves-bait (specs, fitment)** are what earn distribution in car communities. | Shift mix toward Reels + UGC; product-hero stays but isn't the reach engine. |
| 5 | **Posting where reach is lowest, skipping where buyers convert** | Plan §1: Facebook *Page* organic reach is near-dead; the highest-intent buyers are in **FB Groups + forums** Metricool can't post to. | Manual group/forum routine → `docs/facebook-group-forum-sop.md`. |
| 6 | **No owned audience to seed engagement** | Email/SMS list is ~zero, so every launch restarts cold with no warm base to drive early likes/saves that trigger reach. | Add Klaviyo/Shopify Email (already flagged top-priority in plan §3). |

## What "good" looks like for *us* (reframe the scoreboard)

Stop judging a post by likes. For a considered, high-AOV purchase, judge it by:

| Metric | Why it matters here | Rough healthy signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Saves** | A save = a buyer bookmarking a $1K part for later. Best leading indicator of intent. | Saves climbing on product/spec posts |
| **Shares / sends** | Someone tagging a build buddy = free, high-trust reach. Tier-unlock posts should drive this. | Group-buy posts getting shared into chats |
| **DMs / "is this available"** | Direct buying intent — our funnel is DM-heavy. | Track weekly DM volume |
| **Profile visits → link clicks** | Top-of-funnel working = people going from post → bio → store. | Link-in-bio clicks (UTM'd) |
| **Reach / impressions** | The *actual* problem we're solving. Watch trend, not absolute. | Up week-over-week after cadence fix |
| **Revenue by UTM** | The only number that pays the bills. | Per-campaign attribution live |

**Vanity (track, don't optimize):** likes, follower count.

## How to pull the real numbers (for whoever has account access)

I (the assistant) run in an isolated container and **cannot log into Meta/IG**. To turn this
from inference into a real audit, grab the following and paste/screenshot them back:

- **Meta Business Suite → Insights (last 90 days):** reach, engagement rate, follower growth.
- **Top 5 and bottom 5 posts** by reach — and note which content pillar each was (drop / UGC /
  countdown / install). The top-vs-bottom split exposes the content problem instantly.
- **Per-post saves + shares** (Instagram) — not just likes.
- **Posting frequency** last 30 days (are we hitting 4–6/week?).
- **Shopify social-referral revenue** month-over-month.
